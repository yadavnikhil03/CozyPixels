## 🧘 The Sanctuary Experience
CozyPixels is more than just a wallpaper site. It's a productivity tool designed for the mindful web.

- **Immersive Focus Mode**: Every wallpaper can be opened in "Sanctuary Mode"—a full-screen, distraction-free environment.
- **Built-in Productivity**: Includes a minimalist Pomodoro timer to help you manage your focus sessions.
- **Ambient Serenity**: Features high-quality ambient background sounds to mask noise and keep you in the flow.

## 🚀 The Cozy Engine (Browser Extension)
Bring the serenity of CozyPixels directly into your daily workflow.

The **Cozy Engine** replaces your boring default New Tab page with a rotating digital sanctuary.
- **Auto-Sync**: Automatically rotates to a new minimalist vibe every day or every hour.
- **Instant Focus**: Access your focus tools and ambient sounds directly from every new tab.
- **Lightweight**: Zero bloat, just pure serenity.

### 🛠️ How to Install the Engine
1. Visit the [CozyPixels Sanctuary](https://cozy-pixels.vercel.app/).
2. Click the **"Install Cozy Engine"** button to download the bundle.
3. Open your browser's extensions page (e.g., `chrome://extensions`).
4. Enable **"Developer mode"** in the top right.
5. Click **"Load unpacked"** and select the extracted folder.
